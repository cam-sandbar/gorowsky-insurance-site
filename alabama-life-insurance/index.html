Life Insurance for Alabama Families

Life insurance is one of the most important financial decisions your family can make. It ensures that if something happens to you, the people who depend on you are not left with mortgage payments, debts, or lost income on top of everything else. Gorowsky Insurance helps families across Mobile and Baldwin County find the right coverage at a price that fits their budget.

Types of Life Insurance

There is no single life insurance product that is right for everyone. The best policy depends on your age, income, family situation, and financial goals. Glen walks through each option with you and gives a straightforward recommendation based on your actual needs.

  • Term life insurance for affordable coverage over a set period such as 10, 20, or 30 years, ideal for income replacement during working years
  • Whole life insurance for permanent coverage with a cash value component that builds over time
  • Universal life insurance for flexible permanent coverage with adjustable premiums and death benefits
  • Final expense policies for seniors looking to cover funeral costs and end-of-life expenses

How Much Coverage Do You Need

A common guideline is 10 to 12 times your annual income, but the right amount depends on your mortgage balance, number of dependents, outstanding debts, childcare costs, and whether your spouse works. Glen reviews your specific situation and helps you land on a coverage amount that genuinely protects your family without overpaying for more than you need.

When to Get Life Insurance

  • When you get married or start a family
  • When you buy a home and take on a mortgage
  • When you start a business or take on business debt
  • When your current policy is expiring or no longer adequate
  • When you want to lock in lower rates while you are young and healthy
